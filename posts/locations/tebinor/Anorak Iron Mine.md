Anorak Iron Mine is located in the [[Slumbering Hills]] to the northwest of [[Ebonwatch]] on the shard of [[Tebinor]]. Under the control of the [[Vaviel Empire]], the mine provides raw [[Iron Ore]] to the inhabitants of [[Ebonwatch]] to use in construction and [[Smithing]].

* A Tribe of [[Lizardfolk]]
* A Tribe of [[Troglodyte|Troglodytes]]

The Silverlight Family was native to the town of [[Triboar]] and were the family [[Alaric]] the [[Orc|Half-Orc]] originated from.

After [[Pandora Silverlight|Pandora]] gave birth to [[Alaric]] and refused to abandon him, they left [[Triboar]] and lived in a [[Silverlight Cabin|Cabin]] in the [[Neverwinter Woods]].

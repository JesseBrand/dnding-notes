## Never Miss Potential Trouble
[[Exilian]] and [[Malrik Voss|Malrik]] sat quietly at different tables in the [[Black Barrel Inn|Inn]], the low hum of travelers filling the air, when commotion stirred at the city gates. [[Bill]], [[Lynel]], and [[Sir Dixon Fire|Sir Dixon]] had arrived but the [[Vavian Soldiers|Guards]] blocked their entry. [[Sir Dixon Fire|Sir Dixon]], having mysteriously turned blue, was deemed too suspicious to let through, even though his flashlight eyes might have given him away. The [[Vavian Soldiers|Guards]] indicated only a signed letter from the [[Duke Oakfire|Duke]] would convinced them, otherwise [[Sir Dixon Fire|Sir Dixon]] would need to stay outside.

While waiting, [[Sir Dixon Fire|Sir Dixon]] handed [[Bill]] a simple task: buy [[Castle-8]], who was grumpily standing in the center of town, a beer. [[Bill]] rushed inside on the errand, catching [[Exilian]]’s attention. Curious, [[Exilian]] glanced outside, noticing [[Castle-8]], [[Sam Alderleaf|Sam]], and [[Ru Eir Hydrus|Ru]] gathered near the gate, and headed that way. [[Malrik Voss|Malrik]], never one to miss potential trouble, quickly headed out after him.

As the group reassembled near the gate, [[Enphine]] and [[Flicker Stardust|Flicker]] arrived. [[Flicker Stardust|Flicker]] immediately wrapped [[Sir Dixon Fire|Sir Dixon]] in a tight embrace, while [[Enphine]] attempted to make sense of the situation before giving up and joining the others. After a brief discussion, [[The Party]] agreed: their next stop was to rid the [[Gamwater Quarry|Quarry]] of the [[Shadow|Shadows]] that had invested it. [[Sam Alderleaf|Sam]], [[Ru Eir Hydrus|Ru]] and [[Castle-8]], recuperating their losses after a partially succesfull expedition to [[Moldarhim]], decided to remain behind. Reunited, they headed out.
## [[Shadow]] on the road
Halfway towards their destination, a thick cloud of black fog rolled across their path. Sensing danger, [[Enphine]] and [[Sir Dixon Fire|Sir Dixon]] began casting the [[Ritual of the Sun]]. But before the [[Ritual of the Sun|Ritual]] could take hold, [[Flicker Stardust|Flicker]] reached out and touched the cloud.

The fog collapsed into [[Flicker Stardust|Flicker]]’s own shadow and then attacked him.
[[Bill]] reacted immediately, but not fast enough to prevent [[Flicker Stardust|Flicker]] from taking a massive blow. The [[Shadow]] burst apart and swept toward the rest of the group mid‑ritual. [[Exilian]]’s arrows shot cleanly through the writhing darkness. [[Sir Dixon Fire|Sir Dixon]] halted his casting. [[Malrik Voss|Malrik]] unleashed a [[Fireball]], dispersing the last of the shadow. [[Sir Dixon Fire|Sir Dixon]], stubborn as ever, resumed the [[Ritual of the Sun|Ritual]], and the party pressed on.
## The Quarry Awakens
As they neared the [[Gamwater Quarry|Quarry]], a massive cloud of [[Shadow]] loomed ahead, being pushed back as the [[Ritual of the Sun]] radiated outward from [[Sir Dixon Fire|Sir Dixon]] and [[Enphine]]. But as the group descended further, things took a grim turn.

Those who were not actively casting the ritual felt their own shadows begin to stretch, creep, and finally rise from the ground alive. Chaos broke out. [[Enphine]] faltered first; her concentration shattered. As half of the [[Ritual of the Sun]] keeping the party alive failed, the decision to retreat was quickly made. Before they could act however,  [[Sir Dixon Fire|Sir Dixon]] lost his [[Concentration]] moments later, plunging [[The Party]] in darkness. Without the sunlit protection, the surrounding darkness surged. The enormous [[Shadow Monster]] they had encountered before reformed, towering above them.

[[Malrik Voss|Malrik]] fell. [[Enphine]] fell. [[Flicker Stardust|Flicker]] fell. But [[Lynel]], quick and desperate, saved [[Flicker Stardust|Flicker]] from death. As [[Flicker Stardust|Flicker]] gasped back to life, [[Lynel]] whispered urgently for him to climb onto him because [[Lynel]], inexplicably, now had the wings to [[Fly]]. [[The Party]] was stunned by the sight of the flying horse‑man. 

Meanwhile [[Malrik Voss|Malrik]], uncomfortably familiar with the realms of the damned, forcefully rejected the call of death itself and surged back to his feet, after which he attempted to scale the wall himself.

[[Enphine]], revived by a hastily administered berry, raised up her hands and summoned a [[Wall of Force]] to stall the [[Shadow|Shadows]]. But many simply soared over it, and the [[Shadow Monster]] reached across it with disturbingly long limbs.
## Desperate Escape
Realizing the fight was hopeless, [[The Party]] fled toward the towering [[Gamwater Quarry|Quarry]] wall, an eighty‑foot ascent between them and survival.

[[Sir Dixon Fire|Sir Dixon]] took to the air first as a [[Giant Eagle]], bleeding heavily as he climbed into the sky. He swept down to lift [[Bill]], who had astonishingly climbed most of the cliff already.
Behind them, [[Malrik Voss|Malrik]] and [[Exilian]] slipped into [[Invisibility]], evading the eyes of the encroaching [[Shadow|Shadows]]. However [[Enphine]], as she attempted to climb the [[Gamwater Quarry|Quarry]] wall, was intercepted by the [[Shadow|Shadows]] and engulfed by the darkness beneath them. As a final attempt of mercy, [[Lynel]] created a [[Moonbeam]] on top of [[Enphine]], sparing her the reincarnation as a [[Shadow]] herself.

Battered, scattered, and barely alive, [[The Party]] escaped the [[Gamwater Quarry|Quarry]], its horrors echoing behind them. They fell silent as they left their friend behind.

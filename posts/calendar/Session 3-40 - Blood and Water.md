## [[Aboouck]]'s Stuffed Animal
The day in the [[Black Barrel Inn|Inn]] started easily; everyone was drunk before 10 because of extra strong brewed beer and a lot of ruckus coming from the kitchen. [[Aboouck]] was extra frisky today as he had obtained a new stuffed animal, which appeared to be extra special! [[Aboouck]] shot out of the kitchen towards the first friend he could see, [[Sam Alderleaf|Sam]] sitting at the bar. [[Aboouck]] showed him the stuffed animal and asked what she saw.

[[Sam Alderleaf|Sam]], struck by the sight of the stuffed animal and trapped in memory lane, was baffled and stunned for words, freezing in time. [[Aboouck]] told her it's a special animal that changes into your favourite stuffed animal of all time, perhaps from your childhood. Curious as to what other people saw, he ran outside, where [[Castle-8]] was waiting in the snow, unable to get into the tavern, and showed him the stuffed animal too, asking what he saw. [[Castle-8]] saw a stuffed squirrel. [[Aboouck]], super psyched, explained the magic to him. [[Enphine]] showed up with her trusty [[Gnome]], [[Flicker Stardust|Flicker]]. [[Aboouck]], stuck in the kitchen for a long time, hadn't seen what happened to [[Enphine]] and was shocked, but was more interested in what she saw in the stuffed animal, which was a dull stuffed book. [[Aboouck]], not sure which part he should be more confused about ([[Enphine]]'s childhood stuffed animals or her current state) ran back inside to get more [[Bottle of Boundless Coffee|Coffee]].
## Winter Party Outside
[[Lynel]], [[Bill]], and [[Sir Dixon Fire|Sir Dixon]] showed up as well. With both [[Lynel]] and [[Castle-8]] outside, [[Sir Dixon Fire|Sir Dixon]] offered to fetch them a beer and he went inside to grab some. He saw [[Aboouck]] and asked him what he was doing with his Beardy-bear, at which [[Aboouck]] started putting the stuffed animal away protectively. [[The Party]] assembled outside, deciding what to do. [[Sam Alderleaf|Sam]] also offered [[Castle-8]] another beer from his special stash. [[The Party]] decided to head out towards the [[Anorak Iron Mine|Iron Mine]].
## No Climbing Walls
Before leaving [[Ebonwatch]], [[Sir Dixon Fire|Sir Dixon]] gave [[Castle-8]] a grappling hook. [[Castle-8]] immediately wanted to test it out and did so by climbing the outer walls of [[Ebonwatch]], alerting the [[Vavian Soldiers|Guards]]. The [[Vavian Soldiers|Guards]] cut the rope in the midst of [[Castle-8]]'s climbing attempt, making him fall to the ground. [[Castle-8]] questioned the [[Vavian Soldiers|Guards]] as to why they would do such a thing, getting the reply that it is strictly forbidden to climb the walls. [[Bill]] mentioned they should put up a sign to tell people.
[[Sam Alderleaf|Sam]] started to question the [[Vavian Soldiers|Guards]] about what would happen if someone tried to [[Fly]] over the wall, like winged [[Demon|Demons]]. The [[Vavian Soldiers|Guards]] replied, saying they have guard towers with archers for that. [[Sam Alderleaf|Sam]] wasn't convinced by the skills of the guards and thus challenged them to shoot her. After some convincing, they took out a training shot, but soon realized they needed more training to hit this bird. [[Sam Alderleaf|Sam]] flew up to retrieve the grappling hook.
## [[Ian]] Falls Down, [[Ian]] Gets Up Again
[[The Party]] ventured forth towards the [[Anorak Iron Mine|Iron Mine]], meeting their favourite friend [[Ian]]. [[Ian]] greeted them saying, "The [[Society]] welcomes you." [[Sir Dixon Fire|Sir Dixon]] put a [[Goodberry]] in his mouth, followed by some coffee from [[Aboouck]], which gave him shivers, followed by "The [[Society]] welcomes you.", after which [[Castle-8]] tackled him and pushed him to the ground once again. [[The Party]] headed inside.
## Water Under the Bridge
[[The Party]] approached one of their favourite bridges, the one near the whirlpool, only surpassed in popularity by the one at [[Hornwood]]. [[Flicker Stardust|Flicker]] jumped onto [[Castle-8]]'s shoulder and they headed out. [[Sir Dixon Fire|Sir Dixon]] tried to carry [[Aboouck]] but failed terribly. [[Sam Alderleaf|Sam]] came out to rescue them both. [[Enphine]] also tried to beat the current but, again, failed like last time. Whilst everyone else was busy helping her out, [[Aboouck]] came out to save her using [[Telekinesis]] and tossed her into a safe spot.
## Reacquainting with [[Skriss]]
[[The Party]] went through the mines to get to the [[Lizardfolk]] and [[Troglodyte|Troglodytes]] under control of [[Skriss]]. They were stunned to see them arrive; they thought that [[The Party]] was fighting the [[Troglodyte|Troglodytes]] for over a week. In a rush to get rid of the [[Demon]] influence, [[The Party]] moved on. [[Sir Dixon Fire|Sir Dixon]] showed [[Aboouck]] what the place looked like and followed swiftly.

[[The Party]] arrived at the cave system where they were at last week and noticed there was this big black wall in their way. [[Sir Dixon Fire|Sir Dixon]] encouraged [[Flicker Stardust|Flicker]] to have a look at what it might be and encouraged him to touch it. [[Flicker Stardust|Flicker]] started poking at the wall, but other than a cold feeling it seemed like just an icky wall. [[Castle-8]] went on to the next level and started kicking it, but that hurt like hell and made him reconsider.

[[The Party]] headed back to [[Skriss]] to consult on what was going on. [[Sir Dixon Fire|Sir Dixon]] said he felt off since last week and demanded that [[Skriss]] investigate, up to the point he was hitting [[Skriss]]. To prevent any further harm, [[Skriss]] put some kind of [[Mind Blank]] on [[Sir Dixon Fire|Sir Dixon]] in order to calm him down. [[Skriss]] told [[Enphine]] that they should head back to the place where they exited the area last time as they might find their way back inside there. [[Castle-8]] said he would love to, but it's way up in the air and he had no way of getting there. [[Skriss]] gave [[Castle-8]] a potion, which he was hesitant to accept; he thought that she might want to kill him. [[Enphine]] [[Identify|Identified]] it as a [[Potion of Flying]].
## Thank You For [[Fly|Flying]] With [[Enphine]]
[[The Party]] headed outside. [[Castle-8]] noticed that [[Ian]] was standing again and pushed him to the ground once more.

[[Enphine]] looked around to see if she could detect some magic residue hanging around from the last time they were there and noticed that there was something floating in the sky. [[Sam Alderleaf|Sam]] went forth to try and check it out. [[Flicker Stardust|Flicker]] was amazed and took out his spyglass to look at how he was [[Fly|Flying]]. He noticed a meteor come towards him and tried to dodge it. This was in fact not a meteor, but [[Sam Alderleaf|Sam]]'s soon-to-be-scrambled egg; it hit [[Flicker Stardust|Flicker]] full in the face.

[[Enphine]] noticed that she could [[Dispel Magic|Dispel]] the magic keeping them from entering, but it would mean that when she did, there would be a short window for the whole [[The Party|Party]] to enter. So [[Castle-8]] took his [[Potion of Flying]] and ascended. [[Flicker Stardust|Flicker]] started to [[Levitate]] after some harassment from [[Enphine]]. [[Castle-8]] carried [[Enphine]] and [[Bill]] upwards after [[Bill]]'s failed attempt to [[Fly]].

[[Sir Dixon Fire|Sir Dixon]] was mumbling a bit and looked dizzy. [[Aboouck]] thought he might know the trick to snap him out of it and poured some coffee into him. [[Sir Dixon Fire|Sir Dixon]] started shivering and out of nowhere shot off into the sky like a rocket. [[Sam Alderleaf|Sam]] rushed after [[Sir Dixon Fire|Sir Dixon]] to try and save him, but as he descended he fell so fast that she couldn't reach him before he got sucked into the portal that [[Enphine]] had just opened. [[Sir Dixon Fire|Sir Dixon]] tried to [[Wild Shape]] into a [[Giant Eagle]], but due to his [[Mind Blank]] he turned into a skunk, after which he also ended up in the portal.
## Surrounded by [[Giant Toad|Giant Toads]]
The whole party made it into the portal in time but ended up with the same velocity as they entered the cave with when they left last week. They stood in knee-high water, surrounded by [[Giant Toad|Giant Toads]]. [[Aboouck]] and [[Bill]] were very alert and directly noticed their surroundings. [[Bill]] started slashing the [[Giant Toad|Giant Toads]] around him. [[Aboouck]] cast Twinned [[Haste]] on both [[Castle-8]] and [[Sam Alderleaf|Sam]], who immediately flew to their targets to cut them down.

[[Aboouck]] tried to put a [[Goodberry]] into [[Sir Dixon Fire|Sir Dixon]]'s mouth but ended up smudging it on [[Sam Alderleaf|Sam]]'s paws as [[Sir Dixon Fire|Sir Dixon]] was somewhere unknown under water. [[Flicker Stardust|Flicker]] blasted away with his [[Eldritch Blast]] towards the [[Giant Toad|Toads]]. [[Enphine]] started doing her favourite spell, [[Fireball]]. [[Castle-8]] flew up towards a big group above the cliffs with some ballistae and archers, only to be shoved down.
One of the spell-casting [[Troglodyte|Troglodytes]] started casting [[Black Tentacles]], but then something strange happened. From out of the water where the tentacles appeared, a blue figure rose up. He was wearing [[Sir Dixon Fire|Sir Dixon]]'s clothes and equipment and started to cast a [[Wall of Stone]]. Nobody knew who this figure was, but nobody complained about the spell either.
## Let The Bodies Hit The Floor
[[Sam Alderleaf|Sam]] was punching away with her mighty quarterstaff and fists, knocking down [[Troglodyte]] spellcasters one after the other all over the place within the blink of an eye. [[Bill]] was hacking at the big ones and downing them; it was a David and Goliath face-off. [[Enphine]] was hiding tactically under the water to avoid the arrows from the archers while she setup a [[Wall of Fire]]. [[Aboouck]] tried to head out but got swatted down by some nasty-looking [[Troglodyte|Troglodytes]]. The [[Wall of Fire]] and [[Wall of Stone]] were dispelled and a [[Hezrou]] arose from one of the [[Giant Toad]]'s corpses. [[Flicker Stardust|Flicker]] was target practice for the archers and went down as well. [[Sir Dixon Fire|Sir Blue Dixon]] started getting worn down by the [[Giant Toad|Giant Toads]] and [[Hezrou]] and also hit the ground. [[Bill]] was targeted by a bunch of [[Troglodyte|Troglodytes]] as well and hit the ground too.
## Saved by the bell
As the last spellcaster came under pressure, she teleported out together with her bodyguards. Out of nowhere, a big laughter from beneath the floor hollered out and the remaining [[Troglodyte|Troglodytes]] and [[Hezrou]] vanished and got teleported out. It seemed like something or someone wanted to keep [[The Party]] alive for some reason, but what could that be? They noticed tunnels leading down but decided not to explore them at this moment but rather return to [[Ebonwatch]], through the tunnel that had mysteriously opened again.
